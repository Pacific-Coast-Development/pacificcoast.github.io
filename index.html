Procman

A modern visual process explorer for macOS.

Procman helps you understand what's running on your Mac, how processes are related, what they're consuming, and why they exist. Instead of forcing you through a flat list, it organizes your system into a live parent-child tree with the clarity of pstree and the polish of a native Mac utility.

Direct download 3-day free trial macOS 13+

Live process tree

Browse a live hierarchical view of everything running on your Mac.

Deep system insight

Inspect CPU, memory, disk, network, and energy usage at a glance.

Fast workflow

Search, sort, inspect, kill, or restart processes without friction.

Menu bar access

Keep system-wide activity one click away with a configurable applet.

Why it exists

Made for the moments when Activity Monitor isn't enough.

Procman is built for developers, power users, IT professionals, and anyone who has ever wondered what their Mac is actually doing in the background. It turns process relationships into something legible, useful, and even pleasant to explore.
